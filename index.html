Delivery Tracker - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2025

Data Collection and Use

Location Data

Our app requires access to your location information to:

  • Record driving distances for delivery orders
  • Calculate working hours and statistical data
  • All location data is stored locally on your device only and is not uploaded to any servers

Order Data

  • Order information you enter (amounts, distances, times) is stored locally only
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal order data on external servers
  • You can export or delete all your data at any time

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted and stored on your device using iOS standard encryption
  • We do not use any third-party analytics services
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. All data remains on your local device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View all your data at any time
  • Export your data in CSV format
  • Delete all your data
  • Disable location data collection (though this may affect app functionality)

Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device until you choose to delete it. We do not have access to or control over your local data.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by delivery drivers and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app.

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)
  • British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)
  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
